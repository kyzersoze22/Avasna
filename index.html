At Avasna, we support architects, designers, and studios with reliable 3D visualization, architectural planning, and BIM services. Our approach balances design intent with technical accuracy, helping teams communicate ideas clearly and execute projects with confidence.

We work as an extension of your studio, adapting to your workflow, timelines, and project scale. From concept visuals to detailed BIM models, our focus is on consistency, clarity, and dependable delivery.

  • 3D Visualization

    We provide high-quality 3D visualization services for architectural, interior, and design projects, helping teams clearly communicate ideas and intent. Our visuals focus on accurate materials, lighting, proportions, and spatial quality to support presentations, approvals, and design development.

  • Planning & Design

    We support architectural and interior projects with clear, well-structured planning and design solutions. Our work focuses on functional layouts, accurate drawings, and design coordination that align with project requirements, approvals, and execution needs.

  • Working Drawing

    We produce clear and accurate working drawings that support smooth execution on site. Our drawings focus on detailing, coordination, and constructability, helping teams translate design intent into build-ready documentation.

  • BIM Services

    We provide BIM services that support coordinated design and efficient project delivery. Our models are developed with accuracy and clarity, enabling better collaboration, clash coordination, and informed decision-making across project teams.
