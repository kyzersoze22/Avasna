Cala Sol

Cala Sol is a coastal villa designed for relaxation, where architecture merges effortlessly with sea, sky, and silence. Situated on the cliffs of Ibiza, the home embraces a sun-soaked palette, with crisp white surfaces, light woods, and panoramic glazing that opens up to the Mediterranean. From the floating staircase in the living area to the infinity pool blending into the horizon, every element amplifies lightness, calm, and luxurious ease.

A sunlit retreat where the horizon begins at your doorstep

The design process for Cala Sol was driven by the desire to frame the sea from every vantage point. Orientation, material selection, and spatial flow were guided by sunlight and coastal breezes. A restrained palette and open-plan layout foster clarity and connection, while details like built-in furnishings, curated lighting, and handcrafted finishes add warmth.
